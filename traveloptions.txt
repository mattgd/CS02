The six continents are: Africa, Asia, Australia, Europe, North America,
and South America (this game excludes Antarctica).

TRAVEL OPTIONS:

To Africa:
From Asia > plane, boat, car
From Europe, North America, and South America > plane, boat

To Asia:
From Africa, Europe > plane, boat, car
From North America, South America > plane, boat

To Australia:
From Africa, Asia, Europe, North America, South America > plane, boat
From North America > N/A

To Europe:
From Africa, Asia > plane, boat, car
From North America, South America > plane, boat

To South America:
From Africa, Asia, Europe > plane, boat
From North America > plane, boat, car
